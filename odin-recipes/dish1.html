Lasagna

Desc: Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

  • Boil Pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water
  • Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken
  • Combine Cheese & Mix: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  • Layer & Bake: layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasanga noodles and bake until browned and bubble
